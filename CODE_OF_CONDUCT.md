# 📜 Code of Conduct

## **Our Pledge**
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---
## **Our Standards**
**Acceptable:** Welcoming language, respect, constructive criticism.
**Unacceptable:** Sexualized language, trolling, harassment, doxxing.

---
## **Our Responsibilities**
Project maintainers will enforce standards and take action against violations.

---
## **Scope**
Applies to project spaces and public representation.

---
## **Enforcement**
Report violations to the project team. All complaints will be reviewed.
